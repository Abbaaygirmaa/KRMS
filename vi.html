Introduction


A kebele is the smallest administrative unit of Ethiopia similar to a ward, a neighbourhood or a localized and delimited group of people (Keller, 1998).Nowadays Kebele is empowered to handle services like land administration, issuance of ID cards, Marriage certificate, Birth certificates, Policing services, build up small enterprises, land ownership licensing transfer of properties and Kebele court in the area.

Vision

Due to the number of many residents and their associated files,Generally the mission of Kebele is to provide the following Services in efficient and sufficient manner.These are: Registering population, Registering house ,Giving ID card for citizens, Land administration Organizing peoples in small enterprise and etc...

Mission

The general objective of this project is to develop a web-based kebele management system for kebele residence.
